DevOps Interview Questions

  • Could you briefly introduce yourself and share your professional background?
  • What are your day-to-day responsibilities in your current or previous roles?

  • Which AWS services have you worked with? (Examples: EC2, EKS, S3, IAM, VPC, Route 53, Lambda, RDS)
  • Can you walk me through a recent project where you used multiple AWS services together?
  • How do you ensure security and compliance when working with AWS services?
  • What components are required to create a VPN in AWS?
  • How would you provide access to users across three environments?

  • What is state locking?
  • Which Terraform commands have you used?
  • If the state file is lost, how can you recover it?
  • If creating 100 instances with for-each, how would you resolve an issue with one specific instance?
  • How can you resolve and roll back an issue with the current Terraform apply?
  • What is the difference between for-each and count in Terraform?
  • Have you created any Terraform modules? If so, what was the module's purpose and structure?
  • How do you manage dependencies between resources in Terraform?
  • Can you explain the concept of state locking, and how do you achieve it in your projects?
  • What strategies do you use to manage and share Terraform state files?

  • What is ReplicaSet and Replication Controller?
  • Where does the selector work?
  • How would you plan to access an application hosted on an EKS cluster?
  • Which is the most important component of an EKS cluster: the master or the slave?
  • What is etcd, and can you explain more about it?
  • What is the difference between CNI and kube-proxy?
  • How can you store sensitive data in an EKS cluster?
  • What is a headless service?
  • How can you configure a static IP for a service?
  • Why might a PVC remain in a pending state, preventing a pod from starting? How would you resolve this?
  • How would you deploy a StatefulSet without losing state?
  • Can you explain the differences between Secrets and ConfigMaps in Kubernetes?
  • How do you differentiate between StatefulSets and Deployments, and when would you use each?
  • What steps do you follow to upgrade an EKS cluster?
  • When troubleshooting or tracing issues with pods, what is your typical process?
  • How do you set up and manage ingress flow in Kubernetes?
  • Have you worked with Istio or other service meshes? If so, what benefits did it bring to your environment?

  • What is your strategy for merging pull requests? How do you handle large or conflicting changes?
  • Can you explain the difference between rebasing and merging?
  • What steps do you take to resolve merge conflicts?
  • Have you created or customized any GitHub workflows? If so, what purpose do they serve?

  • Can you share some basics about Docker?
  • What is your typical flow for building, tagging, and pushing a Docker image to a registry?
  • How do you handle environment-specific configurations in Docker?

  • What monitoring tools have you used? (Examples: Grafana, Prometheus, CloudWatch, etc.)
  • Have you created any custom Prometheus queries? If so, what were they for?
  • Could you describe your setup for centralized logging, such as using EFK (Elasticsearch, Fluentd, and Kibana)?
  • Have you created custom dashboards in Grafana? If so, what kinds of metrics did you track?
  • What types of dashboards have you created, and what metrics were monitored?

  • How do you manage credentials and secrets in your CI/CD workflows?
  • Have you implemented any automated testing or validation steps in Jenkins?

  • Which tools do you use for security in your projects?
  • How do you handle vulnerability scanning for Docker images or infrastructure as code?
  • What practices do you follow to secure cloud resources, containers, and CI/CD pipelines?

  • How do you stay up to date with new tools and best practices in DevOps and cloud computing?
  • What is your process for troubleshooting and resolving production issues quickly and effectively?
  • Can you share an example of a script you created that provided valuable results?
